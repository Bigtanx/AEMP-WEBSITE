Our Programs:

Empowering Through Action
At the African Economic Mandate Project, our programs are at the heart of our mission to foster economic development, nurture entrepreneurship, and uplift communities across Africa. Each year, we host a series of impactful programs designed to promote career growth, educational advancement, and spiritual enrichment.

Rejoice Africa Concert

February
Celebration of faith and culture, the Rejoice Africa Concert brings together communities for a night of inspiring music and worship. This annual event is a highlight of our calendar, showcasing the rich diversity and talent within our region.
