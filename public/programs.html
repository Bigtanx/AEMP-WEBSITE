Our Programs:

Empowering Through Action
At the African Economic Mandate Project, our programs are at the heart of our mission to foster economic development, nurture entrepreneurship, and uplift communities across Africa. Each year, we host a series of impactful programs designed to promote career growth, educational advancement, and spiritual enrichment.

Rejoice Africa Concert

February
celebration of faith and culture, the Rejoice Africa Concert brings together communities for a night of inspiring music and worship. This annual event is a highlight of our calendar, showcasing the rich diversity and talent within our region.

Careerwise Seminar

April & October (Online Weekly Sessions)
Designed for professionals and business leaders, the careerwise seminar epuips participants with the tools they need to excel in thier careers. Now available as a weekly online sessions, this program offers flexible learning opportunities for individuals at all stages of thier careers.

Anniversary Celebration

May
Join us each May as we celebrate another year of growth, impact, and community at AEMP. Our anniversay celebration is a time to reflect on our achievement and set goals for the future

Educational Drant for Secondary & Primary Schools

Commited to supporting the next generation, we provide educational grants to pupils and students in primary and Secondary schools within our host communities. This program helps enusure that young minds have access to the education they need to thrive.

Annual Secondary Schools Quiz Competition

Encouraging academic excellence, our Annual Secondary Quiz Competition brings together students from across the region to test thier knowledge and compete for scholarships and prizes

Glory Night

August
A night of spiritual renewal and worship, Glory Night is an annual gathering where we come together to seek divine guidance and celebrate the blessings in our lives

Thanksgiving Day

December
As the year draws to a close, our Thanksgiving Day event is an opportunity to express our gratitude for the many successes and milestones we've achieved. its a time for reflection, praise, and community

ICT Skill Acquisition Program

Inaugural Edition: 2019
In response to the growing demand for technology skills, our ICT Skill Acquisition Program provides participants with the training needed to excel in the digital economy. This program also offers interships and apprenticeships as part of our commitment to driving innovation

Medical Outreach

Dedication to improving the well-being of our communities, our Medical Outreach program offers free healthcare services to those in need. This initiative is a vital part of our effort to promote holistic development and well-being

Connect with Us

Stay connected with us through our social media channels. Follow us for the latest updates, events, and inspiring stories.
