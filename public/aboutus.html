Who We Are

Founded in 2009, The African Economic Mandate Project is a faith-based, not-for-profit organization dedicated to promoting economic development and uplifting communities across Africa. We believe that true transformation begins with empowering individuals — whether it’s through entrepreneurship, career development, education, or community service.
Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small initiative into a dynamic organization with a diverse range of programs and a reputation for making a meaningful impact in the lives of thousands.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see a thriving Africa where every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential, contributing to a society characterized by economic prosperity, ethical leadership, and a deep sense of community.

Our Mission

At AEMP, our mission is to empower lives, foster economic growth, and support the holistic development of individuals and communities. We achieve this through a combination of educational programs, business development initiatives, and charitable outreach, all guided by our core values.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We conduct our activities with honesty and transparency, staying true to our commitments and principles.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, setting high standards and continuosly seeking to improve

Faith

Our faith underpins our work, guiding our decisions and inspiring us to serve with compassion and dedication

Resourcefulness

We are innovative and proactive, finding creative solutions to challenges and making the most of available resources.

Connectedness

We believe in the power of community and collaboration, working together to achieve shared goals

Looking Into The Future

As we move forward, we are excited to expand our impact by transforming our international Headquarters into a Technology and Innovation Hub. This new initiative will serve as an incubator and accelerator for emerging businesses, positioning us at the forefront of economic development in Africa.

Promote New Technologies

By embracing and promoting new and emerging technologies, we aim to drive innovation and economic growth.

Empower Entreprenuers

Providing tools, resources, and mentorship to help startups scale and compete globally in a digital age.

Foster Collaboration

Building networks between innovators, investors, and institutions to solve Africa’s most pressing challenges.
