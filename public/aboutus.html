Who We Are

Founded in 2009, The African Economic Mandate Project is a faith-based, not-for-profit organization dedicated to promoting economic development and uplifting communities across Africa. We believe that true transformation begins with empowering individuals — whether it’s through entrepreneurship, career development, education, or community service.
Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small initiative into a dynamic organization with a diverse range of programs and a reputation for making a meaningful impact in the lives of thousands.
