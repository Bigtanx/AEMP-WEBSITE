    About AEMP-Ignite Africa

    AEMP-Ignite Africa is a faith-based initiative focused on restoring Africa’s economic dignity by raising vision-driven professionals and entrepreneurs. We empower people through training, mentorship, scholarships, outreach programs, and economic empowerment initiatives.

    AEMP was inaugurated in May 2009, inspired by a vision received in February 2007. Since then, we’ve impacted thousands of lives across Africa and beyond.

    What does It reflects our mandate to ignite an economic revolution in Africa following the wave of spiritual revival—empowering individuals to become global economic giants.

    We run a range of programs, including: Career & Business Seminars (practical strategies for professionals and entrepreneurs).
    Educational Scholarships & Grants (for students and young leaders).
    Medical & Community Outreach (impacting underserved communities).
    Mentorship Programs (career, business, faith, and leadership guidance).....

    Our programs serve students, career professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders across Africa and the diaspora.
    Membership & Participation

    You can join our membership community by filling out the online form on the website. Membership gives you access to mentorship sessions, networking, and opportunities for training and engagement.

    Most of our activities are free to attend, thanks to the support of donors and partners. Some specialized programs may require a small registration fee.

    We welcome volunteers and partners passionate about faith, business, and economic development. Visit the “Join Us” page to learn more about partnership opportunities

    GloryNight is our annual flagship gathering where members, partners, and friends come together to celebrate impact, share testimonies, and cast vision for the future.

    Follow us on social media, subscribe to our newsletter, or join our WhatsApp community for weekly inspiration, member spotlights, and mentorship Q&A.
    Support & Giving

    We are sustained through donations, sponsorships, and partnerships. Every contribution directly supports scholarships, outreaches, and capacity-building initiatives.

    You can give securely through our website’s “Donate” page. No gift is too small—it all contributes to changing lives and empowering Africa’s future leaders.
